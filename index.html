Why registration is required?

Whole content (words, learned words for each user) is stored on the server.

I need to identify each player/user. Believe me I tried a couple of different approaches. And this social login idea was the quickest and easiest. If someone will have the better idea, please contact me.

  • When the user login from another device, the user will be able to resume where he left of from. Nowadays people change phones quite often. In the end everything is handled on server side.
  • It opens the possibility to create a Scoreboard. You can compete with other players. Or even with friends.

Why it's English English app?

The main idea is to teach users to think in English as well as speak in English. This habit is really useful especially when you try to talk with someone.

If you try to create a sentence in your head, in your native language, and translate it into English. You're creating some kind of hesitation. Which may be stressful or even block you from speaking. The main difficulty is to remove this habit of thinking in your native language when you're speaking in English. The goal of this application will help you do that.

At the beginning it might be hard, but your effort will pay off.

Is there anyway I could add my own words?

At this point you can't create your own content. But I do have an idea for creating your content. If you're interested or have an idea, please contact me. It's really important to learn users expectations and discuss with them.

Is there an offline mode?

At this point an offline mode isn't planned. This feature needs some rethinking of the play and scoring features. It might be complex from a synchronization/logic perspective.
